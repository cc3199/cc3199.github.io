Deploying Collection Site

Please double-check your config.yml page and your theme.yml page before performing this step. It would also be wise to type bundle exec jekyll s into your terminal, click the server link, and double-check your full website.

Once your collection is completely finished and ready to be published to the live webstie, type rake deploy into your terminal. This will run the Jekyll build command which may take quite a bit longer than Jekyll serve. Once finished, your complete site will be output in the '_site' directory.

For now, please let Chelsea Pincock know that your collection is finished, and she will take care of the rest.
